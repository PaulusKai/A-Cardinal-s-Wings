# Ending and Final Thoughts

Regardless of how much you read, I want to thank you for giving a damn about this book.

It took me many hours in Ocean beach, Golden Gate Park, the Library, and (obviously) in class.

There were a lot of moments where I felt like giving up this project – that’s when those random kind words hit me hard. Although those people didn’t think much to tell me “you can do this”, I was surprised I kept going back to those words of encouragement when I didn’t feel like writing.

I couldn't have survived if it weren't for everyone around me: friends, teachers, parents, coaches, and mentors.

Once again, thanks for reading. I’m going to update this book from time to time (perhaps others can put their thoughts in here too, just email me at contact.justinlui@gmail.com) In the meantime, 加油 and fly high!
